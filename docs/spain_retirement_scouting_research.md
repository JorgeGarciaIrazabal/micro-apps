# Spain Retirement Scouting: Sub-€50,000 Plot Alternatives

This report presents a detailed analysis of five affordable, family-oriented suburbs in Spain where urbanized building plots are available for **around or under €50,000** (for 350–500 m² or larger). It focuses on three locations **inside Madrid Province**, one "Madrid-border cheat-code" in **Guadalajara**, and the top-value option in **Granada**.

For a family with a **€2,000,000 stock portfolio** and a **€300,000 build budget**, these options trigger **€0 Wealth Tax** (under a 50/50 spousal split) and provide standard local schools, sports facilities, and shopping centers under 25 minutes away.

---

## 💰 1. Wealth Tax & Legal Structure

For a total wealth of **€2,300,000** (a €2,000,000 stock portfolio and a €300,000 primary residence):

*   **Madrid & Andalucía (100% Tax Rebate):** **€0 / year** (the state Solidarity Tax also does not apply since wealth is under €3,000,000).
*   **Granada (Andalucía):** **€0 / year** — Andalucía applies a **100% regional Wealth Tax rebate (bonificación)** since 2022, so the full cuota is eliminated regardless of asset level (provided net wealth stays under the €3M Solidarity Tax threshold, which it does under a 50/50 split). Note: regions like **Segovia (Castilla y León)** and **Zaragoza (Aragón)** do **not** offer the 100% rebate and would incur real Wealth Tax at this wealth level, so they are excluded from this analysis.
*   **Guadalajara (Castilla-La Mancha):** **~€1,465 / year (~€122 / month) total for the family** if owned 50/50.

### 💡 How the 50/50 Split Works (And the Crucial Pre-Move Rule)
Spain's Wealth Tax is strictly **individual**. There is no joint wealth tax return for married couples.

*   **Official Legal Citations:**
    *   **Individual Taxation:** **Artículo 7 de la Ley 19/1991, de 6 de junio, del Impuesto sobre el Patrimonio** establishes that the tax has a strictly personal and individual character. Each spouse must file their own separate return (*Modelo 714*).
    *   **Matrimonial Property & Joint Accounts:** **Artículo 7 (Atribución de bienes y derechos)** dictates that assets are attributed to spouses based on their legal marital regime or co-ownership. Under a community property regime (*bienes gananciales*) or for assets held in joint names (such as bank/brokerage accounts in *cotitularidad*), the assets are attributed **50% to each spouse**, unless a different ownership share is legally proven.
    *   **Binding Tax Authority Rulings:** The Spanish tax authority (**Dirección General de Tributos - DGT**) has repeatedly confirmed this 50/50 split for joint financial accounts in binding consultations such as **DGT V2369-21** and **DGT V2218-24**, which state that joint account funds are divided equally between titulars for Wealth Tax purposes.
    *   **Allowances & Deductions:** **Artículo 28 (Mínimo exento)** grants each taxpayer an individual tax-free allowance of **€700,000** (which can be modified regionally). **Artículo 4.Uno (Exención de vivienda habitual)** grants each taxpayer an additional deduction of up to **€300,000** for their primary residence.
*   **How it works legally:** 
    *   If your stock portfolio (e.g., Betterment or brokerage accounts) is held in a **joint account** (with both of your names, such as a JTWROS account in the US), *Hacienda* will automatically attribute **50% of the value to you and 50% to your spouse** based on the joint contract titularity.
    *   This splits your €2,000,000 portfolio into two individual portions of **€1,000,000** for tax purposes.
    *   In a region with a €700,000 general exemption (like Castilla-La Mancha) and a €300,000 primary residence exemption, each of you can apply these deductions individually, which reduces your taxable base to a very low level (or €0), resulting in negligible tax.
*   **⚠️ The Critical Pre-Move Rule (Avoiding Gift Taxes):**
    *   **Do not wait until you move to Spain to split your assets.**
    *   If you transfer funds or stocks from a single-owner account (e.g., in Jorge's name) to your spouse's account (Marissa's name) or to a joint account *after* you become a Spanish tax resident, *Hacienda* can classify this as a **Gift (Donación)**. 
    *   In many regions of Spain (including Castilla-La Mancha), gifts between spouses are not 100% tax-free and will trigger **Gift Tax (Impuesto sobre Sucesiones y Donaciones)** under **Ley 29/1987, de 18 de diciembre**.
    *   **The Solution:** Open joint accounts and transfer your stocks into them **BEFORE you move to Spain** (while you are still US tax residents). Under US tax law, spousal transfers are completely tax-free (due to the unlimited marital deduction), and Spain has no jurisdiction to tax the transaction. You will then arrive in Spain already holding joint accounts, which is fully legal and triggers no Spanish taxes.
    *   *Note on retirement accounts (401k/IRA):* US retirement accounts are strictly individual and cannot be made joint. However, under Spanish law, pension plans (including US 401ks/IRAs) are generally exempt from Wealth Tax during the accumulation phase until you begin receiving payouts.

## 🏡 2. Suburban Location Analysis & Local Links

### 📍 Madrid Province Options (Guaranteed €0 Wealth Tax)

#### 1. Villarejo de Salvanés (Madrid) — *The A-3 Commuter Corridor*
A lively, historic town in south-east Madrid. It has its own active community life and is highly connected via the A-3 highway.
*   **Plot Price:** **€40,000 – €50,000** for **300 m² to 500 m²** urban solar plots (fully serviced, ready to build).
*   **Local School:** **[Colegio Santa Elena](https://www.colegiosantaelena.com/)** (a private bilingual concertado school offering Infantil, Primaria, and Secondary) or the public **CEIP Nuestra Señora de la Victoria**.
*   **Shopping Mall:** **[CC H2O Rivas](https://www.h2occ.com)** (featuring all major shops, cinemas, and restaurants) is a **22-minute drive** straight down the A-3 highway.
*   **After-School & Sports:** The *Polideportivo Municipal Mariano Díaz* offers tennis, swimming, and municipal sports. Padel courts are bookable via **[Playtomic](https://playtomic.io)**.
*   **Commute Times:**
    *   *To Madrid Center (Puerta del Sol):* 40–45 mins.
    *   *To CC H2O Rivas:* 22 mins.
    *   *To Local Schools:* 2–5 mins.

#### 2. Colmenar de Oreja (Urtajo / Valle de San Juan) (Madrid) — *American-Style Subdivisions*
Spacious residential subdivisions of detached homes located just south of Chinchón. Unlike Chinchón, they do not have strict heritage architectural rules, so prefab builds are fully welcome.
*   **Plot Price:** **€30,000 – €45,000** for massive **800 m² to 1,000 m²** flat, urbanized plots.
*   **Local School:** The public **CEIP Apis Aurelia** in Colmenar (10 mins), or the highly rated concertado **Colegio Apóstol Santiago** in neighboring Aranjuez (18 mins).
*   **Shopping Mall:** **Aranjuez Commercial Area** (featuring CC E.Leclerc, supermarkets, and local shops) is an **18-minute drive** away. **CC Plaza Éboli** (Pinto) is 25 minutes.
*   **After-School & Sports:** The *Complejo Deportivo Municipal* of Colmenar de Oreja or various sports/padel clubs in Aranjuez (like *Club de Padel Aranjuez*).
*   **Commute Times:**
    *   *To Madrid Center:* 50 mins.
    *   *To Aranjuez Center (Leisure/Shops):* 18 mins.
    *   *To Local Schools:* 10–18 mins.

#### 3. Nuevo Baztán (Eurovillas) (Madrid) — *The East Commuter Belt*
One of the largest residential developments in Europe, composed entirely of detached single-family chalets in a grid-like layout.
*   **Plot Price:** **€40,000 – €50,000** for a **500 m² to 1,000 m²** urban plot.
*   **Local School:** **[Colegio Internacional Eurovillas](https://www.colegiointernacionaleurovillas.com)** (private/concertado) or the public **CEIP Juan de Goyeneche** (both located directly inside the development).
*   **Shopping Mall:** **[CC H2O Rivas](https://www.h2occ.com)** is a **24-minute drive** away.
*   **After-School & Sports:** The *Centro Deportivo Eurovillas* has tennis, padel courts, and playgrounds within the urbanization.
*   **Commute Times:**
    *   *To Madrid Center:* 45 mins.
    *   *To CC H2O Rivas:* 24 mins.
    *   *To Local Schools:* 2–5 mins.

### 📍 Guadalajara Option (Madrid Border Cheat-Code)

#### 1. Galápagos (Montelar) (Guadalajara) — *The Gated Community*
A residential town directly adjacent to El Casar, sitting on the northern border of Madrid.
*   **Plot Price:** **€45,000 – €55,000** for massive **800 m² to 1,400 m²** flat, urbanized plots in the *Montelar* development (featuring 24h private security).
*   **Local School:** **[Colegio Montealbir](https://www.colegiomontealbir.es)** (a bilingual private/concertado school in El Casar, 5 mins away) or local public schools in Galápagos.
*   **Shopping Mall:** **[CC Plaza Norte 2](https://www.plazanorte2.com)** (San Sebastián de los Reyes, a premier shopping hub north of Madrid) is a **20-minute drive**.
*   **After-School & Sports:** Municipal sports center in El Casar (5 mins), and internal community sports courts in Montelar. Padel courts bookable via **[Playtomic](https://playtomic.io)**.
*   **Commute Times:**
    *   *To Madrid Center:* 45 mins.
    *   *To CC Plaza Norte 2:* 20 mins.
    *   *To El Casar Schools:* 5 mins.
*   **Tax:** ~€122/month Wealth Tax (split 50/50).

### 📍 Granada Option (Cheapest Cost of Living)

#### 1. Las Gabias (Híjar) (Granada) — *The Granada Commuter Belt*
A flat, modern residential expansion with wide streets and new family homes, located in the metropolitan area south of Granada.
*   **Plot Price:** **€45,000 – €50,000** for a **350 m² to 450 m²** urban plot in Híjar.
*   **Local School:** The public **CEIP Pilar Izquierdo** in Híjar, or the private bilingual **[Colegio Internacional Granada](http://www.colegiointernacionalgranada.com)** in nearby Otura (10 mins).
*   **Shopping Mall:** **[CC Nevada Shopping](https://www.nevada.shopping)** (the largest mall in Andalucía, featuring FNAC, Primark, Zara, and dining) is a **10 – 12 minute drive**.
*   **After-School & Sports:** The *Polideportivo Municipal de Las Gabias* and local padel academies. Padel is bookable via **[Playtomic](https://playtomic.io)**.
*   **Commute Times:**
    *   *To Granada Center:* 15 mins.
    *   *To CC Nevada Shopping:* 10–12 mins.
    *   *To Local Schools:* 2–10 mins.
*   **Tax:** **€0** Wealth Tax.

## 🚦 Recommendations & Next Steps

1.  **If you want to stay strictly in Madrid Province:** Focus on **Villarejo de Salvanés**. It offers a plot for €45k, standard public schools, a very easy A-3 highway drive to CC H2O (22 mins), and **€0 Wealth Tax** (the lowest monthly expenses among the Madrid options).
